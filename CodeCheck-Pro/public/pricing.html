Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, we'll prorate the remaining time on your current plan. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for educational institutions. Contact our sales team to learn more about our educational discounts and how to apply.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and can also arrange for purchase orders and invoicing for educational institutions.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. No credit card required to start your trial. You can explore all features before making a decision.

How do you count students?

Students are counted as unique users who submit assignments for grading within a billing period. You can add or remove students as needed throughout the academic year.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your plan until the end of your current billing period.
